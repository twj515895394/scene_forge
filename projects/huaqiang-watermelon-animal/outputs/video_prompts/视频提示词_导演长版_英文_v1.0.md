## 🧭 Global Consistency Rules

- **Character Consistency**: Honey Badger remains a cool boss, Bulldog is vulgar and irritable, Marmot is neurotic and snobbish. No face-changing across segments.
- **Scene Consistency**: Pink scooter on the left, long wood counter in the center, Bulldog and rack area on the right. Do not cross the 180-degree axis.
- **Performance Consistency**: Honey Badger's grass straw, tilted leaning pose, Bulldog's stunned freeze, and Marmot's stretched neck crying must be tracked across segments.
- **Motion Consistency**: Strictly inherit `ACT_CHAIN_01 / 02 / 03`, especially hanging the helmet, leaning on the counter, flipping the scale, and exposing the magnet.
- **Audio Continuity**: Inherit cicada silence points, toothpick-picking, melon-patting, cleaver-chopping, scale-flipping, juice explosion, and Sa-Ri-Lang sound effects/music switches.
- **Blocking Continuity**: Each segment must define left-right positions, depth, and allowed paths to avoid teleportation.
- **Prop State Continuity**: Retro scooter and helmet, cleaver, electronic scale, magnet, and watermelon debris must be continuously visible and track their states.
- **Cinematic Language Continuity**: The narrative rhythm curve of Low-frequency setup -> Vacuum confrontation -> High-frequency blast -> Absurdist wrap-up must be kept intact.
