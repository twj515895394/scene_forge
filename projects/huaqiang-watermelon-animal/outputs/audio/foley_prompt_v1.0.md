# 拟音与音效提示词包 v1.0 (foley_prompt_v1.0.md)

本文件提供用于在外部 AI 音效生成平台（如 ElevenLabs, AudioCraft, ElevenLabs Sound Effects 等）生成本片各类拟音与卡通效果音 (Foley & SFX) 的提示词组合，已排除任何血腥与写实物理穿透音效。

## 🔊 1. 卡通载具与物理动作音效 (Cartoon Physics & Actions)

### 🛵 粉色踏板车引擎 (VGU_01 / S01_02)
*   **Prompt (SFX)**：
    ```text
    A high-pitched, plastic-sounding vintage scooter engine hum, 'putt-putt-putt-putt' cartoon scooter idling rattle, toy-like gasoline engine exhaust sputter, clean studio recording.
    ```

### 🍉 拍西瓜空心木音 (VGU_02 / S02_04)
*   **Prompt (SFX)**：
    ```text
    A hollow, deep wooden knock, 'donk, donk' sound of tapping a ripe hollow pumpkin or thick wood board, natural reverb, short decay.
    ```

### 🪑 竹椅粗鲁滑动 (VGU_05 / S05_01)
*   **Prompt (SFX)**：
    ```text
    A loud wooden scraping screech, 'creeeak' of a bamboo chair leg dragging forcefully on dry sandy dirt ground, harsh sliding sound.
    ```

### ⚖️ 电子秤翻转碎裂与弹簧 (VGU_09 / S09_05)
*   **Prompt (SFX)**：
    ```text
    A plastic box smashing and shattering on the ground, 'clack-crack-smash', accompanied by a funny cartoon metallic coil spring bouncing 'sproing' effect.
    ```

## 🔊 2. 卡通物理冲突与高潮音效 (Cartoon Conflict & Climax)

### 🔪 大砍刀剁案板与振颤 (VGU_08 / S08_02)
*   **Prompt (SFX)**：
    ```text
    A heavy metallic knife blade chopping hard into a thick wood table, 'thwack', immediately followed by a high-pitched metal blade wobble vibrating sound 'boooing-n-g-g' ringing out.
    ```

### 🌊 西瓜高压液态大爆炸 (VGU_10 / S10_05)
*   **Prompt (SFX)**：
    ```text
    A massive high-pressure water balloon bursting, wet splash kaboom, huge liquid explosion, high velocity juice spraying, gushing water splash, no metal cutting.
    ```

    ```text

    ```
