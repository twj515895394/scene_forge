本文件提供用于在外部 AI 音乐生成平台（如 Suno, Udio 等）生成本片背景音乐 (BGM) 的提示词组合与结构描述。

## 🎵 1. 主题配乐一：西部孤狼牛仔登场 (VGU_01 ~ VGU_02)
*   **适用场景**：平头哥骑摩托车滑入、下车、解扣挂帽踱步问价。
*   **音乐风格**：Spaghetti Western (意式西部片风格), Morricone style.
*   **BGM 提示词 (Prompts)**：
    ```text
    【Style/Tags】: spaghetti western soundtrack, acoustic slide guitar, slow dry whistling, rhythmic slow handclaps, dusty trail atmosphere, solitary, minimal, 72 bpm, vintage analog tape saturation.
    【Description】: A slow, spacious acoustic slide guitar strumming a lonely minor chord progression. A dry, clean whistling melody floats above, evoking a quiet standoff in a hot desert. Gentle, rhythmic handclaps count the slow beats. Very minimal instrumentation, high tension, dry acoustics.
    ```
*   **混音建议**：主乐器偏左声道，口哨声置中，带入大排档夏日蝉鸣的沙沙底噪。

## 🎵 2. 主题配乐二：焦灼大片对峙 (VGU_03 ~ VGU_05, VGU_08 ~ VGU_09)
*   **适用场景**：商贩轻蔑、言语交锋、老板怒起站立、拔刀剁案板、推瓜掀秤。
*   **音乐风格**：Cinematic Action Thriller (电影级对峙紧张感).
*   **BGM 提示词 (Prompts)**：
    ```text
    【Style/Tags】: cinematic thriller, low cello drone, double bass tension, rising violin tremolo, slow ticking percussion, suspense, dry, heavy atmosphere, 80 bpm.
    【Description】: A deep, vibrating low cello drone holds a continuous tense note. Slow, nervous violin pizzicato notes pluck in irregular patterns. A heavy double-bass slides down slowly, building immense dark pressure. The music slowly escalates in pitch and speed, creating a suffocating stand-off atmosphere.
    ```
*   **混音建议**：大提琴低频拉满，小提琴颤音在中高频游走，压抑环境背景噪声。

## 🎵 3. 主题配乐三：大核爆后的荒诞交响悲歌 (VGU_11)
*   **适用场景**：劈瓜果肉汁大爆炸之后，土拨鼠小弟抱着老大胖狗头嚎哭萨日朗。
*   **音乐风格**：Absurd Classical Opera Parody (荒荒歌剧交响反差).
*   **BGM 提示词 (Prompts)**：
    ```text
    【Style/Tags】: grand symphonic tragedy parody, epic classical opera chords, weeping soprano strings, dramatic swell, slow tempo, melodramatic, orchestral irony.
    ```
*   **Description**：
    ```text
    A highly dramatic and weeping orchestral string section playing a grand, tragic theme. Exaggerated classical opera tragedy chords swell to a parody climax. Melodramatic cellos and crying violins lament, mock-heroic brass fanfare briefly blares, creating an absurdly dramatic atmosphere that parodies grand tragic movie endings.
    ```
*   **混音建议**：声场拉到极致宽（Wide Soundstage），模拟歌剧院大厅混响（Large Hall Reverb），为土拨鼠的破音哭嚎提供夸张的史诗级背景垫底。
