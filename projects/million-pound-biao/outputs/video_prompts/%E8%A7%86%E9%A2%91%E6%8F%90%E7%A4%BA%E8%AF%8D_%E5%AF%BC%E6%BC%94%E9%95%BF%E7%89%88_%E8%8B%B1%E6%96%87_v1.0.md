## 🎨 I. Global Render & Consistency Rules

### 1. Character Anchors
*   **Biao Ge (biao-ge)**: 5-head-tall, rounded-rectangle silhouette. Wearing a matte worn-out black faux-leather jacket, a bright red polo shirt underneath with the collar popped up, a thick gold chain necklace, and gold-framed aviator sunglasses. He carries a black zippered clutch bag under his right arm. His facial expression remains a deadpan, stoic "dead-fish face" throughout.
*   **Wu Zong (wu-zong)**: 5-head-tall, chubby roly-poly silhouette. Wearing a dark satin suit vest, and a massive, flashing gold watch on his left wrist. He is a snobbish, groveling manager.
*   **A Gang (a-gang)**: 6.5-head-tall, paper-thin silhouette. Wearing a crooked bow tie and a server's vest, with a white towel over his shoulder. He has a triangular face and high, narrow shoulders.
*   **Ma Dashuai (ma-dashuai)**: 5.5-head-tall, slightly hunched old peasant. Wearing a washed-out coarse grey buttoned shirt, trousers rolled up twice, and black cloth shoes. He holds a hot roasted sweet potato emitting volumetric white steam.

### 2. Spatial Blocking
*   **Zone B (Left side counter area)** & **Zone B/C Channel**: The primary area for Wu Zong and A Gang.
*   **Zone C (Right side window-side round table)**: The dining and card-revealing area for Biao Ge, with Ma Dashuai sitting at a nearby table.
*   **Zone A (Center entrance & Roman clock)**: The main entrance door area with a Roman wall clock hanging above, where the final door-greetings, card-reaction shots, and shotgun misfire occur.

### 3. Cartoon Expressive Physics & Safety Boundaries
*   **Animation Physics**: Biao Ge's cheeks puff up cartoonishly like balloons when slurping. A Gang's jaw drops 3 times its normal length (Jaw-drop gag) when shocked.
*   **Soot Face (No-injury Gag)**: The plaster dust falling from the ceiling covers Wu Zong and A Gang's faces in a solid white chalky layer, leaving black clean rings around their blinking eyes (classic cartoon soot face), with absolutely no physical injuries.
