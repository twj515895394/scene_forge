*   **设计要点**：皮夹克、红领POLO衫、蛤蟆镜、金链子、手拿包。多视角设定板，包含吃面、淡定、受惊表情。
*   **Prompt (英文投喂)**:
    > **Prompt**: 3D Pixar style character concept sheet of a middle-aged Chinese man named Biao Ge. He is about 5头身, slightly chubby with a confident but deadpan face. He is wearing an oversized retro black faux leather jacket, a bright red popped-collar polo shirt underneath, and a thick gold chain necklace. He has gold-rimmed aviator sunglasses on and is clutching a black leather pouch under his arm. The sheet shows a symmetrical multi-view model (front, 3/4 side, back view) and a grid of expressive facial expressions including deadpan, slurping noodles with chopsticks, and comedy shock. Clean studio background, clay model rendering, Unreal Engine 5 render style, cinematic lighting. --ar 16:9
*   **Prompt (中文参考)**:
    > **提示词**: 3D皮克斯动画电影风格的角色设定图，中年中国男子彪哥。约5头身，微胖，自信又一本正经的死鱼脸。身穿松垮的黑色人造革夹克，内搭立领大红色POLO衫，脖子上戴着一根粗金链子。脸上戴着金丝蛤蟆镜，腋下夹着一个黑色手拿包。设定板展示对称的多视角模型（正面、3/4侧面、背面）以及一组表情网格（包括死鱼脸、用筷子狼吞虎咽吸面条、滑稽震惊）。干净的摄影棚背景，粘土渲染质感，虚幻引擎5渲染风格，电影级柔和光影。 --ar 16:9

Clean studio background, clay render, Unreal Engine 5 style, octane render. --ar 16:9
