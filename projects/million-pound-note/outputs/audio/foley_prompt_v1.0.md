# 拟音与特效音生成提示词 (Foley Prompts) v1.0

本文件定义了《百万英镑》经典餐厅结账改编场景中 18 个镜头所需的非写实卡通物理音效、角色拟音与环境音效提示词。

---

## 🔊 卡通物理动作音效 (Cartoon Physics SFX)

### 📌 Foley 1：小二下巴脱臼拉伸 (Jaw Drop Stretch - SH15)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > A funny cartoon metallic spring springy bounce sound, cartoon boing effect, pitch shifting upwards, followed by a rubbery squeaky stretch sound. High dynamic range. No realistic body cracking or wet tearing sounds. Clear and clean.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 滑稽的卡通金属弹簧跳动回弹声，卡通 boing 音效，音高向上偏移，随后是橡胶拉伸的吱吱声。高动态范围。禁止任何写实的骨头碎裂声或湿黏撕裂声。清晰干净。

### 📌 Foley 2：眼镜震飞空中翻转 (Monocle Spin in Air - SH15)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > High-pitched metallic spin and twirl, a glass lens spinning fast in the air, light ringing metal resonance, followed by a soft catch sound. High quality.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 高频金属旋转声，玻璃镜片在空中快速飞转，轻微鸣响的金属共鸣，随后是软性的接住声。高品质。

### 📌 Foley 3：茶匙茶罐滞空蹦极 (Airborne Teacup Overshoot - SH12)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > Comedic slide whistle sliding up and then down, cartoon wup-wup-wup sound indicating gravity suspension, followed by a clean ceramic and metal clinking sound on tablecloth.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 滑稽的滑音笛向上后向下倾斜，卡通 wup-wup-wup 声音，指示重力悬浮，随后是干净的瓷器与金属在桌布上的碰击声。

### 📌 Foley 4：小二原地打滑旋转 (Windup Spin - SH17)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > Comedic squeaky shoe rubber sliding fast on wooden floor, repetitive spinning squeaks, followed by a mechanical windup spring sound and a happy ding.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 滑稽的橡胶鞋底在木地板上快速打滑的“吱吱”声，连续旋转的擦地板摩擦音，随后是机械发条上紧声和一个清脆的“叮”。

## 🍴 动作拟音与细节音效 (Action Foley)

### 📌 Foley 5：大口吸面与咀嚼 (Devouring Noodles - SH07)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > Comedic exaggerated wet sucking and slurping sound, high-speed pasta ingestion, quick breathing gasp, funny cartoon eating effect. No realistic choking.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 滑稽夸张的湿润吸溜声，高速吸面条声，快速换气呼吸，有趣的卡通进食音效。禁止写实噎住或咳嗽。

### 📌 Foley 6：面饼刮擦碗底 (Flatbread Wiping Bowl - SH09)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > A very squeaky clean friction sound, flatbread rubbing against empty clay pottery bowl in a circular motion, producing a sharp high-frequency squeak (like wiping a clean window).
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 极其清脆干净的摩擦声，白面饼在空陶碗内壁转圈刮擦，产生尖锐的高频“吱吱”声（类似于擦拭干净的窗户）。

### 📌 Foley 7：星芒高光闪烁 (Star Glint - SH14)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > A magical high-pitched crystalline shimmer chime, thin needle-like metallic glint sound, sparkling light wind-chime effect.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 魔法般的晶莹高频闪烁鸣响，细针状的金属闪光声，闪烁的轻盈风铃效果。

## 🚪 空间与环境音效 (Environment SFX)

### 📌 Foley 8：沉重橡木门开合 (Oak Doors opening/closing - SH03 / SH18)
*   **Prompt (English)**:
    > Heavy wooden door slow squeaking hinge opening, low wood creak, followed by wind blowing. For closing: a heavy low-frequency wood slam thud, echo in a large hall, absolute cutoff of indoor reverb.
*   **Prompt (中文)**:
    > 沉重橡木门缓慢嘎吱开门声，低沉的木质摩擦，随后是风掠过。对于关门：沉重的低频木门拍击闷雷声，在大厅内回荡，室内混响被瞬间切断。
