## 1. 全局渲染规则 & 渲染风格 (Global Rendering Rules)

### 🔴 中文全局渲染规则 (Chinese Global Rules)
*   **视觉风格**：3D 迪士尼/皮克斯动画电影风格，PBR 材质渲染。粘土微孔皮肤质感，高精度球衣布料网格，半金属拉丝质感的电视之星 18 足球。
*   **光影氛围**：索契体育场深蓝色夜空背景，上方打下几道粗壮的巨型探照灯冷色体积光柱（丁达尔效应），漫天飘洒轻微的毛毛细雨水汽。
*   **180度射门轴线锁定**：主机位必须架设在球场右半区（X轴正向半区）。葡萄牙进攻方向（球门）始终在画面的**右侧**，西班牙防守方向在画面的**左侧**。足球运行轨迹为从**左下向右上**飞行。
*   **角色外号唯一性**：禁止使用球员真实姓名。全片仅包含“总裁”（红衣 7 号）、“鸭哥”（灰衣橙手套门将）、“白色人墙”（5名西班牙白衣防守队员）、“红衣队友”（红衣葡萄牙球员）。
*   **无水印约束**：禁止生成任何广播级字幕、平台水印、比分板 UI 元素或球场广告牌文字。

### ⚪ English Global Rules
*   **Visual Style**: 3D Disney/Pixar animated feature style, PBR texture rendering. Stylized clay skin with fine pores, high-precision mesh fabric jersey, semi-metallic brushed texture of the Telstar 18 soccer ball.
*   **Lighting & Atmosphere**: Deep blue night sky over Sochi Stadium, several thick pillars of cool-toned volumetric light beams (Tyndall effect) casting down from giant floodlights, light misty drizzle in the air.
*   **180-Degree Shooting Axis Lock**: The main camera must remain on the right side of the field (positive X-axis hemisphere). The attack direction (goal net) is always on the **right side** of the screen; the defense direction is on the **left side**. The soccer ball travels from the **bottom-left toward the top-right**.
*   **Pseudonym Enforcement**: Never use real player names. Use "President" (red jersey #7), "Yage" (grey jersey with bright orange gloves), "White Wall" (5 Spanish defenders in white), and "Red-clothed teammates".
*   **Negative Constraint**: No broadcasting subtitles, platform watermarks, scoreboard UI, or legible advertising board text.
