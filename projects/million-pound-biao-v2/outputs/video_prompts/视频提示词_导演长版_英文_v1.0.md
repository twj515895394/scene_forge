## 🎨 I. Global Consistency Rules

### 1. Character Consistency Anchors
*   **Biao Ge (biao-ge)**: 5 heads tall, slightly chubby rounded-rectangle silhouette. Wearing a washed faded grey suit jacket, tucked in with an oversized red POLO shirt (collar popped up), wearing aviator sunglasses (left lens wrapped with black electrical tape), clutching a vintage black zippered leather handbag under his right arm. Facial expression is permanently a deadpan "dead-fish face," trying desperately to keep his dignity.
*   **Frost (frost)**: 6.5 heads tall paper-thin silhouette, triangular face, snobbish shifty eyes. Wearing a black waiter vest, a slightly crooked red bow tie, with a yellow-white tea towel draped over his high, narrow, hunched shoulders.
*   **Foreman (foreman)**: 7.5 heads tall, slender figure, wearing a fitted tuxedo, wearing a single monocle, arrogant facial expression with a sneering mouth.
*   **General Manager Clemens (clemens)**: 5 heads tall plump man, wearing a high-end double-breasted tuxedo, wearing gold-rimmed round glasses, looks extremely nervous and sweaty, with a tendency for hysterical over-thinking.
*   **Boss (boss)**: 5.5 heads tall elderly English shopkeeper, hook-nosed, wearing small round spectacles, looks extremely shrewd, greedy, and miserly.
*   **Maggie (maggie)**: 5.5 heads tall elderly English woman, sharp pointed chin, wearing a headscarf, shifty eyes, extremely snobbish.
*   **Potato-Elder (potato-elder)**: 6 heads tall kindly elderly English peasant, wearing a thick, old knitted sweater, slightly hunched, acting slowly, dining on a large plate of potatoes at the neighboring table (Zone D).

### 2. Blocking & Spatial Continuity
*   **Screen Left (Zone B Counter & Zone B/C Walkway)**: The active zone for Frost, Foreman, General Manager Clemens, Boss, and Maggie.
*   **Screen Right (Zone C Window Table)**: The exclusive territory for Biao Ge. He sits on a dark leather chair, legs crossed, with a frosted glass window behind him. The wall clock at Zone A aligns diagonally with him.
*   **Screen Center (Zone A Main Entrance & Wall Clock)**: A giant baroque brass mechanical wall clock hangs right above the double door, with its black second hand ticking heavily. This area is also used for deep bows and guest send-offs.
*   **Outside snowy street**: Cozy warm light spilling onto London winter street, heavy volumetric snow storm, ground covered with slippery ice sheet.

### 3. Cartoon Physics & Comedic Safety Limits
*   **Cartoon Physics (Squash & Stretch)**: Frost's jaw drops and stretches down 2.5 times towards his chest in comic shock (Jaw-drop), then snaps back with a wood-peg popping sound. Biao Ge's cheeks puff up cartoonishly like a balloon while masticating steak and raw scallions.
*   **Comedic Injury Safety (No-bleed)**: Biao Ge's ending slip on the icy pavement features extreme cartoon smear motion on legs, landing flat on his back into a snowbank. Rubbing his waist triggers yellow lightning bolt pain waves, completely avoiding realistic fractures, blood, or screams.
