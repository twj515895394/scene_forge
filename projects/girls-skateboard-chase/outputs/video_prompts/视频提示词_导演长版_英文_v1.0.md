## 2. Global Render & Generation Rules

1. **Visual Style Constraints**:
   3D animated film concept art, Pixar Pixar render style, highly detailed PBR textures, volumetric lighting, rich color scripting, cinematic atmosphere, 8k resolution. (Warm, vibrant, high-end 3D feature animation render).
2. **Character Consistency Anchors**:
   * **Girl A (Energetic Speed Specialist)**: 18-year-old Chinese girl, high ponytail, dark chestnut long hair, large bright brown almond eyes. Wears an ivory white tight athletic crop tank top (neon bright orange trim) and high-waisted charcoal black cargo pockets shorts. Wears a bright white open-face helmet (orange and charcoal cloud pattern print), riding her orange/charcoal checkerboard "CHASE" board (yellow polyurethane wheels, matte black trucks).
   * **Girl B (Calm Technical Trick Specialist)**: 18-year-old Chinese girl, black straight bob hair with straight bangs, calm deep black almond eyes. Wears a sage green tight short-sleeve crop T-shirt (dark navy speed stripes) and high-waisted midnight navy pleated sports skirt. Wears a matte cold grey open-face helmet (deep blue and mint green tech line graffiti), riding her blue-and-mint-green dual-split "FUTURE" board (ice blue wheels, titanium silver alloy trucks).
3. **Spatial & Axis Line Control (Blocking)**:
   * Double yellow lines down the center of the asphalt road serve as the relationship axis. Girl A is locked to the left (outer bend), Girl B to the right (inner bend).
   * In all curves, the camera is positioned low on the inner curve looking outward. This locks the "A on Left, B on Right" layout and strictly prevents crossing the 180-degree axis line.
4. **Global Negative Constraints**:
   * **No Storyboard Layout Traces**: No storyboard grids, frame margins, black split lines, camera tracking arrows, shot number tags (e.g. [S01]), or rough sketch textures.
   * **Safety Redline**: No realistic blood, bone fractures, flesh wounds, or expressions of intense physical agony. All crashes are handled via safe sliding/rolling roll recovery.
   * **No AI Artifacts**: Avoid watermarks, text, limb distortions, floating random debris, or template-like camera movements.
